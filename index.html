About this project

A kinetic motion piece built for a 360° campaign — delivered across social feeds, digital OOH, and paid media. Every frame was designed to work on mute and with sound.

The challenge was maintaining visual consistency across aspect ratios (9:16, 1:1, 16:9) while keeping the energy alive at every cut point. Motion timing was mapped against the audio score from the first frame.
